# The Longevity Charity — Draft Framework

**Working title:** *Longevity for Everyone, Not Just the Wealthy*

---

## The Problem

Most disease is age-amplified. Cancer, heart disease, Alzheimer's, diabetes — these aren't mysterious random events. They're downstream consequences of biological processes that we can observe, measure, and slow.

The science of *why* we age — and *how* to slow it — has advanced dramatically in the last decade. But that knowledge stays locked behind paywalls, expensive protocols, and jargon. The people who need it most — people in poverty, on benefits, in food deserts — are the last to access it.

**This charity exists to change that.**

---

## The Core Insight

Every human body faces the same fundamental problems:

1. **Chronic inflammation** — immune system stuck in "on" mode, driving most age-related disease
2. **Oxidative stress** — cellular damage from free radicals, pollution, stress, poor sleep
3. **Nutritional gaps** — even in first-world countries, most people are deficient in essentials
4. **Mitochondrial dysfunction** — the energy engines of your cells degrading over time
5. **Declining neuroplasticity** — the brain's ability to adapt and repair diminishing

These aren't edge cases. They affect *everyone*. The question isn't *whether* these problems apply to you — it's how early and how aggressively you address them.

---

## The Approach

We recommend a **tiered protocol** — starting with what's universally accessible and expanding from there.

---

### Tier 1: The Universal Foundation (~£15-30/month)

**For everyone. Accessible. Evidence-backed.**

| Supplement | Why | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Omega-3 fish oil (EPA+DHA 1000-2000mg) | Reduces systemic inflammation, supports brain health | Strong — RCTs for CVD, depression, neuroinflammation |
| Vitamin D3 + K2 (4000-5000IU D3) | Most people in northern latitudes are deficient; modulates immune function | Strong — deficiency linked to autoimmunity, poor immune function |
| Magnesium (glycinate or threonate) | Soil depletion means most diets are low; cofactor in 300+ enzymatic reactions | Moderate — sleep, anxiety, blood pressure |
| Multivitamin/mineral | Covers baseline nutritional gaps from modern food processing | Moderate — fills gaps, not a substitute for good food |
| Probiotic (diverse strains) | Gut health drives systemic inflammation; microbiome affects everything | Moderate — emerging evidence for immune and mental health |

**Why these first:** Address the most universal problems — inflammation, nutritional deficiency, oxidative stress — at the lowest cost and widest accessibility. Each one has a strong safety profile.

---

### Tier 2: The Expanded Protocol (~£50-80/month)

**For people who want more and can afford it.**

| Supplement | Mechanism | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Curcumin (with piperine) | Potent anti-inflammatory; NF-κB pathway inhibition | Strong — comparable to NSAIDs in some studies, without the GI risks |
| CoQ10 (100-200mg) | Mitochondrial support; CoQ10 declines sharply after 40 | Moderate — shown to improve cardiovascular function, slow functional decline |
| Alpha-lipoic acid | Antioxidant that regenerates other antioxidants (vitamin C, glutathione) | Moderate — nerve health, insulin sensitivity |
| Resveratrol (250-500mg) | Sirtuin activator; mimics aspects of caloric restriction | Moderate — human trials showing improved vascular function |
| Spermidine | Autophagy inducer; promotes cellular cleanup | Emerging — strong mechanistic basis, early human data |

---

### Tier 3: Advanced Protocols (~£100-200+/month)

**For people working with a healthcare provider who understands the underlying science.**

This tier involves compounds that require more supervision:
- Selegiline (MAO-B inhibition, dopaminergic support)
- Memantine (NMDA modulation, neuroplasticity)
- Berberine (AMPK activation, glucose metabolism)
- Estradiol (in appropriate populations — hormone optimization)
- Senolytic protocols (fisetin, quercetin, dasatinib combinations)

*These are not for everyone. They require baseline testing, monitoring, and often medical supervision.*

## What We Are NOT

- We are not selling products
- We are not making medical claims
- We are not suggesting anyone stop their existing medications
- We are not a replacement for a doctor

## What We Are

A charity that aggregates the best available science on healthy aging — free to access, regularly updated, written for normal people — and uses it to give everyone, regardless of income, a fighting chance against the biological decline that modern life accelerates.

---

## The Science Is Real

This isn't "anti-aging" hype. This is the underlying biology of aging — inflammation, oxidative damage, mitochondrial decline, autophagy impairment — being addressed with compounds that have peer-reviewed human evidence.

The researchers working on this aren't fringe. Aubrey de Grey. David Sinclair. Jennifer Doudna's group. The SENS Research Foundation. Alzheimer's clinical trials targeting neuroinflammation. Immunotherapy trials. Rapamycin trials in otherwise healthy adults.

**The tools exist. They should be available to everyone.**
