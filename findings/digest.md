## 🚨 Top Story: First Human Cellular Rejuvenation Trial

### ER-100 Gets FDA Clearance
**Source:** [Labiotech.eu](https://www.labiotech.eu) | March 6, 2026

**What:** Life Biosciences received FDA Investigational New Drug (IND) clearance for **ER-100** — the **first-ever human clinical trial** of partial epigenetic reprogramming therapy.

**Mechanism:** Uses Yamanaka factors (OCT4, SOX2, KLF4) to rejuvenate cells **without** making them pluripotent (avoiding cancer risk).

**Why It Matters:**
- Moves cellular reprogramming from mouse studies to humans
- First targeted cellular rejuvenation approach in clinical trials
- Could pave the way for aging reversal therapies

**Status:** Trial recruitment expected soon. We'll monitor for results.

**Action:** Track trial progress — may inform future charity research pipeline.
