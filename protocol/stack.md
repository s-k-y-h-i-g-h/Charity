## Mechanisms & Evidence

### Metformin
- **Mechanism:** AMPK activation, mTOR inhibition
- **Evidence:** Strong epidemiological data, ongoing trials (TAME, RAP PAC)
- **Status:** Core compound

### Rapamycin
- **Mechanism:** mTOR inhibition
- **Evidence:** Robust animal data, human trials ongoing
- **Status:** Core compound (see RAP PAC trial)

### Selegiline
- **Mechanism:** MAO-B inhibition, neuroprotection
- **Evidence:** Parkinson's data, emerging longevity research
- **Status:** Active monitoring for new research

### Memantine
- **Mechanism:** NMDA receptor antagonist, neuroprotection
- **Evidence:** Alzheimer's data, cognitive enhancement
- **Status:** Active monitoring

### IDRA-21
- **Mechanism:** AMPA receptor positive allosteric modulator
- **Evidence:** Cognitive enhancement (preclinical)
- **Status:** Active monitoring for human data

### CBD (Cannabidiol)
- **Mechanism:** CB1/CB2 receptor modulation, anandamide reuptake inhibition, anti-inflammatory
- **Evidence:** Strong human data for anxiety, sleep, pain; emerging for neuroprotection
- **Status:** Active (200-300mg daily, isolate) — User reports significant mood/sleep improvement
- **Note:** Isolate preferred for THC-free, legal, consistent dosing
