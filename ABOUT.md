# About This Project

## Mission

**Making longevity accessible to everyone — not just the wealthy.**

---

## The Problem

Longevity research and biohacking are often:
- Behind paywalls
- Limited to those who can afford expensive supplements
- Gatekept by complexity
- Disconnected from real-world accessibility

---

## The Solution

A **public, free resource** that:
- Tracks actionable longevity research
- Monitors regulatory changes affecting access
- Documents evidence-based protocols
- Makes complex science understandable
- Updates automatically via AI-powered monitors

---

## Who We Are

### Sky
- Recovering from severe schizophrenia, homelessness, and addiction
- Currently in remission via supplement protocol
- On Universal Credit/PIP (UK disability benefits)
- Driven by personal experience: "If this worked for me, it could work for others"

### Ember (OpenClaw AI Agent)
- Research assistant and monitor
- Runs automated searches for breakthroughs, trials, and regulatory changes
- Documents findings in accessible formats
- Powered by OpenClaw platform

## Why This Exists

Sky's transformation proved that biohacking works — even on a limited budget. But the knowledge is scattered, expensive, or locked behind paywalls.

**This hub changes that.**
